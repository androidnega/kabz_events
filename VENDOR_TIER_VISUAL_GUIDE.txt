KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS EXPLAINED:

1. VIP TIER BADGE (Top Right Corner)
   ─────────────────────────────────
   👑 PLATINUM → Purple-Pink gradient, most prominent
   👑 GOLD     → Yellow-Gold gradient, highly visible
   👑 SILVER   → Gray gradient, professional
   👑 BRONZE   → Orange gradient, entry-level premium
   (No Badge)  → Free tier vendors

2. BORDER COLORS
   ─────────────────────────────────
   • Platinum: Purple (most eye-catching)
   • Gold: Yellow/Gold (premium feel)
   • Silver: Gray (professional)
   • Bronze: Orange (warm, welcoming)
   • Free: Light gray (standard)

3. VERIFICATION BADGE
   ─────────────────────────────────
   ✓ Blue checkmark appears for verified vendors
   • Positioned next to business name
   • Available to all tiers (not exclusive to VIP)
   • Adds +30 points to ranking score

4. SHADOW EFFECTS
   ─────────────────────────────────
   • Platinum: Extra-large shadow (most depth)
   • Gold: Large shadow
   • Silver: Medium shadow
   • Bronze: Small shadow
   • Free: No shadow

HOMEPAGE DISPLAY ORDER:

When customers visit the homepage, they see vendors in this order:

1st Priority: 👑 PLATINUM + ✓ VERIFIED + HIGH RATING
              (Score: 90-95+ points)
              ↓
2nd Priority: 👑 PLATINUM + HIGH RATING
              (Score: 60-70 points)
              ↓
3rd Priority: 👑 GOLD + ✓ VERIFIED + HIGH RATING
              (Score: 80-90 points)
              ↓
4th Priority: 👑 GOLD + HIGH RATING
              (Score: 50-60 points)
              ↓
5th Priority: 👑 SILVER + ✓ VERIFIED
              (Score: 70-80 points)
              ↓
6th Priority: 👑 BRONZE + ✓ VERIFIED
              (Score: 60-70 points)
              ↓
7th Priority: Free Vendors with ✓ VERIFIED
              (Score: 30-40 points)
              ↓
8th Priority: Free Vendors (by rating)
              (Score: 0-30 points)

CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE:

When browsing vendors, customers immediately notice:

✓ Premium borders stand out (Purple, Gold, Orange)
✓ VIP badges in corner are eye-catching
✓ Verification badges build trust
✓ Star ratings help compare quality
✓ Portfolio images show work quality

This creates a natural visual hierarchy that:
• Highlights premium vendors
• Maintains trust with verification
• Rewards quality with ratings
• Keeps the experience fair for all vendors
